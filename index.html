About Us

MYLOU is an Indonesian rock band originating from Jakarta comprised of Bayu Avrila (lead vocals, pianist, and guitarist), Rizal Umaternate (guitarist), Mon Wahyuda (drummer) and Ahmad Oktaviansyah (bassist).

MYLOU’s songs try to relate the person facing the real world; an anthem that speaks both real-life moments and real-time problems. MYLOU’s music tries to put some balance between positive energies and emotional thought that bring the listener feels… “Okay, I know the answer now”.

In 2021 they will release their first album with their first single “Change of Plan”. They also released “Melawan”, the only song in Bahasa that released in “Kompilasi Kansas”. Their album will release as the third single of the album release.
